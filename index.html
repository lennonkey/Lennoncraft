How to Install

  1. Download and install Minecraft Forge.
  2. Download the Lennoncraft modpack from CurseForge.
  3. Place the modpack file in your Minecraft 'mods' folder.
  4. Open Minecraft and select the Forge profile.
  5. Enjoy the adventure!

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I play Lennoncraft in multiplayer?
A: Yes! Lennoncraft fully supports multiplayer, so you and your friends can enjoy the modpack together!

Q: Will Lennoncraft work with my existing mods?
A: Lennoncraft is designed to work with the base game and can be used with most other popular mods. However, we recommend testing compatibility before adding any major mod.

Q: How do I access the dungeons and new biomes?
A: Simply explore the world to find hidden portals or caves that lead to dungeons. Each biome and dungeon is hidden throughout the world.
